What Should I Do If Someone Is Having a Heart Attack?

Here's what you should do if you or someone you are with has any symptoms of a heart attack, even if they don’t seem serious:

  • Call 911 immediately

    Call 911 here

  • Sit or lie downwhile waiting for the ambulance and loosen any tight clothing.

  • Stay calmAnxiety increases the heart’s need for oxygen and is known to worsen a heart attack

  • CPR:

    Begin chest compressions at a rate of 100 compressions per minute (or 25 compressions in 15 seconds). After 30 compressions, begin rescue breathing.
